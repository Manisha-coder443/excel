CMA COURSES DESCRIPTION

Course Description
In this globalized world. organizations require professionals such as Cost and Management Accountant (CMA) who have specialized knowledge on business strategy and value creation

CMAS are increasingly contributing towards the management of scarce resources like funds, land, risk analysis. developing Cost Control Mechanism, etc and helping the organization in strategic decision-making process

In nutshell. CMA not only helps corporate in managing scarce resources but optimum utilisation of these resources so as to maximize profitability of corporate
CMA/CWA exam in India is conducted by the Institute of Cost and Management Accountancy of India (CMA). set up by an act of parliament. The institute was established in 1944 and later in 1959, it became an official government body

1. Managing the process for optimum utilisation of scarce resources
2. Managing employees effectively
3. Efficiently managing funds (e. the capital of the business)
4. Facilitating in strategic decision-making 5. Implementing Cost effective business process
6. CMAS contribute in making legitimate profiles of the business
7. Helps in sustainable. growth expansion of business
